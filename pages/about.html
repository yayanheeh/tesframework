About Yayan he'eh Framework
Welcome to Yayan he'eh Framework

Yayan he'eh Framework - is a free and open source HTML mobile framework to develop hybrid mobile apps or web apps with iOS or Android (Material) native look and feel. It is also an indispensable prototyping apps tool to show working app prototype as soon as possible in case you need to.

The main approach of the Framework7 is to give you an opportunity to create iOS and Android (Material) apps with HTML, CSS and JavaScript easily and clear. Yayan he'eh Framework is full of freedom. It doesn't limit your imagination or offer ways of any solutions somehow. Yayan he'eh Framework gives you freedom!

Yayan he'eh Framework is not compatible with all platforms. It is focused only on iOS and Android (Material) to bring the best experience and simplicity.

Yayan he'eh Framework is definitely for you if you decide to build iOS and Android hybrid app (Cordova or PhoneGap) or web app that looks like and feels as great native iOS or Android (Material) apps.
